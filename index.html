About

Welcome to Lynx, the official digital home of the Computer Science Department at Mar Ivanios College. Established as a hub for innovation and learning, our department prides itself on fostering academic excellence and technological expertise. With a mission to prepare students for the challenges of the modern digital world, we offer cutting-edge programs and hands-on experiences in software development, data science, and emerging technologies. Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and vibrant student community come together to create an environment of collaborative growth. Lynx serves as a gateway for students, alumni, and enthusiasts to explore departmental news, academic resources, and upcoming events. Join us in shaping the future of technology, one idea at a time.
